My name is Angela, a freshman student-athlete studying Computer Science at Dartmouth College.

I was originally born in China, but immigrated to Vancouver, Canada at age 8. As such, I speak three languages fluently: Mandarin, English, and French. I think it is my love of learning languages and hunger to learn more that have led me to my love for computer languages and coding.

Only 4 months after having written my first ever line of code, I have taught myself several computer languages and platforms out of interest, including Python, Java, R, Javascript/CSS/HTML, Swift 4, and Xcode.

For me, UI development bridges human connections through AI. UI design takes into consideration the user's point of view, what the users need, and how to best achieve their desired results. It's a multifaceted way to view the users not just as people who come across your code, but active subjects who experience what you create. My goal is to make things that can help people, create impact, and help us discover more about our world.

I would love to take my passion for coding, strengths in collaboration, and excitement for UI design to DALI. I want to be in a fast-paced environment where I will be actively challenged and learn new things. Most importantly, DALI is a place where I can work together with like-minded people and grow together. I'm so excited to work with DALI because in every sense, DALI is the ideal place for my dream of learning more things.

Fun fact: I love making memes and sharing them with people. I am currently working on an app that can help people easily design and make memes on the fly to share with friends.
